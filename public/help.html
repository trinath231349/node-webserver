We are proud to announce the 10th Edition of the Nutmeg League this year. We are trying to raise awarness and support Maailu orphanage (www.maaillu.in). Krishi Development Society NGO (www.kirds.org) and a local little league. It is all about fun, fitness, sportsmanship and a good reason to help and support organizations that are thriving to create opportunities for next generation. We have more than 350 players and 50 volunteers registered across CT. Please come and join us on the event dates. Please take a moment and visit the websites above to get insigts into activities of those organizations.
